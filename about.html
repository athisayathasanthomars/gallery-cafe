Gallery Cafe

At Gallery Cafe, we've been proudly serving the community of Colombo 6, Wellawatte, since our inception, with a commitment to delivering an exceptional dining experience. Our restaurant is more than just a place to eat; it's a space where culinary art meets hospitality. We believe in the power of good food to bring people together, and our menu reflects a diverse range of flavors inspired by both local and international cuisines. With a focus on using fresh, high-quality ingredients, we aim to create dishes that are not only delicious but also a feast for the eyes. Join us and become a part of the Gallery Cafe family, where every meal is a masterpiece.
